Cymbidium Orchid

One of the most exquisite orchids found in the Eastern Himalayas!

Growth & Care

Cymbidium Orchids thrive in cool temperatures (10-25°C) with indirect sunlight. Water once a week and ensure well-draining soil.

Flowering Season

Blooms from winter to early spring, making it a perfect addition to your home or garden during festivals.

Unique Features

Known for its long-lasting, fragrant blooms. Ideal for cut flowers, decorations, and gifting.
